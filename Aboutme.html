About Me

Hello! I'm Korotoumou Sidibé, an agronomy graduate with a passion for integrating technology into agriculture. With my expertise in agronomy and my ongoing journey in web development, I aim to transform and modernize Mali's agricultural sector.

I'm currently advancing my skills in web development, having gained proficiency in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Sass, and becoming advanced in JavaScript. Through this blend of agronomy and web development, I hope to drive impactful change in Mali's agriculture by creating innovative tech solutions that improve productivity, sustainability, and accessibility for farmers and stakeholders.

Recently, I launched an agricultural consulting agency where I bring my expertise in agronomy to assist and guide farmers and agricultural businesses. By leveraging technology, I aim to bridge knowledge gaps, increase efficiency, and foster sustainable practices that can contribute to the growth of our agricultural communities.

I'm excited to continue this journey, expand my skillset, and make a meaningful impact in the world of agriculture.
